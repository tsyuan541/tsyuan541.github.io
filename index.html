About Seattle Parks for Kids

Seattle Parks for Kids is an organization dedicated to improving the quality of city parks. Many city parks are in great need of maintenance, cleanup, and safety improvements.

We need parks to be a place for the entire populace of Seattle to gather, mingle, and interact to foster a safer and environment for all.
